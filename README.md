# Onset AI

Onset AI is an AI-powered onboarding platform designed to help teams get new hires and customers up to speed faster and more effectively.

## What it does

Onset streamlines the entire onboarding process in one place, from task management through to training content creation.

## Core features

- **Task assignment and tracking:** Assign onboarding tasks to individuals and monitor completion across your team in real time
- **Learning paths:** Build structured, sequential learning journeys tailored to different roles or teams
- **Team management:** Organise users into teams and manage onboarding at scale
- **AI training video creation:** Upload a screen recording and Onset automatically generates a written script, a step by step guide, and a voiceover overlay, turning raw recordings into polished training content in minutes
- **Content library:** Training videos and guides can be added directly to learning paths or projects for easy access

## Tech stack

Python, TypeScript, Vercel, Render, Cloudflare
